Naruto Shippuden

Naruto is one of the older anime series that is well known for it's action packed battles, insanely smooth animations, exciting plot, and comedic moments. It revolves around a character named Naruto whose goal is to become the Hokage, which is like a president, but he goes through various problems and trials which make it hard for him to achieve that goal. The world of Naruto is one of ninjas and powers with the theme of an ancient kind of elemntal abilities. Other characteristics and plot involve the power of friendship and interal conflicts or battles between organizations and countries.

My Fav Characters in Shippuden
